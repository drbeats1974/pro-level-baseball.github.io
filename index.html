Our Mission

The Ross Experience 24 exists to empower student-athletes with the tools, confidence, and guidance needed to succeed beyond the field. We develop disciplined athletes, strong students, and future leaders.

The Ross Advantage

The Ross Experience 24 goes beyond resources and information. We provide mentorship, accountability, and personalized guidance to help student-athletes succeed in sports, education, and life.

Accountability

Building habits, discipline, and systems that create consistent growth.

Guidance

Helping athletes navigate academics, recruiting, athletics, and life decisions.

Leadership

Developing confident leaders prepared for their future.

Programs & Services

Academic Excellence

Academic planning, accountability, study strategies, and GPA improvement.

Athletic Growth

Performance development, training opportunities, and athlete evaluations.

Leadership Development

Life skills, confidence building, career readiness, and personal growth.
