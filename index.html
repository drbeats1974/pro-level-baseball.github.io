Our Mission

The Ross Experience 24 exists to empower student-athletes with the tools, confidence, and guidance needed to succeed beyond the field. We develop disciplined athletes, strong students, and future leaders.

Programs & Services

Academic Excellence

Academic planning, accountability, study strategies, and GPA improvement.

Athletic Growth

Training opportunities, performance development, and athlete evaluations.

Leadership Development

Life skills, confidence building, career readiness, and personal growth.
